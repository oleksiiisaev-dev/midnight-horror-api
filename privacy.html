Privacy Policy – Midnight Horror Mods

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Midnight Horror Mods is an unofficial mod manager for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. Not affiliated with Mojang AB or Microsoft.

Data We Collect

  • Google AdMob — displays ads, may collect device identifiers. EEA/UK users see a consent dialog first.
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics (screen views, app opens).
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports for debugging.
  • Google Play Billing — subscription purchases handled entirely by Google Play.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or personally identifiable information.
  • No data sold to third parties.

Minecraft Files

The App writes mod files to your Minecraft folder using Android Storage Access Framework. No files are uploaded to our servers.

Children

This app is rated Teen (13+) and is not directed at children under 13.
